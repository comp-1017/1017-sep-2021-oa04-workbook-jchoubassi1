Creating Navigational Links Within a Website

There are several techniques to navigating within a website. The semantic tag for navigation is the <nav> tag. You can navigate through your website by using relative addressing. For our examples we will use the unordered list tag <ul>. Our examples with consist of:

  • Page to page within the same folder
  • Staying on the current page
  • Going to a specific place on current page (on page navigation)
  • Going to a specific place on another page (Choosing Apples)

Navigational menus are placed in your <header> semantic area. They may also be placed in your <lt> area.
